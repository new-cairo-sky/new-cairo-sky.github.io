About

I am a formally trained graphic designer working at the intersection of technology, science and art. After over a decade of developing brands and websites in the the commerical ad and interactive agency world, I redirect my efforts towards more meaningful work. In 2006 I co-founded and managed the successful brand-launch of an ecological product design company that sought to produce cradle-to-cradle designer office products. In 2015 I joined a small group of scientists working to reconceptualize the scholarly, peer-review publishing system. Along the way I've maintained a fine-arts practice and an independant publishing imprint. Working as an artist, I've published numerous books and have exhibited internationally. Most recently, I co-launched Library Stack: a research archive and database of contemporary artists serving libraries and museums across the country.
